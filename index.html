# About Me

Former Teaching Assistant at North South University
Currently appointed as Research Assistant at North South University
Research Interest: Computer Vision, Data Sciecne, Data Mining and Machine Learning
Key Skill Set: Coding in Python, Java and JavaScript, Laravel, Django, Arduino, SQL, Tableau.


# Short Biography


I have pursued my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from the passion for exploring this field. With the aim, I have the focus to engage in a research career in the field of Computer Vision, Data Science, and Machine Learning. My undergraduate capstone and thesis project is based on a combined application of Computer Vision and Data Mining techniques to detect the road surface condition of Bangladesh. I have worked as a Research Assistant from 2017-2019 in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in a project of "Online Sewage Treatment Plant Monitoring". I worked in the project as a Web and Back-end developer and applied data mining techniques on the acquired data. Later, I joined a research project funded by the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh under the supervision of the Department of Environmental Science Management of North South University for the Coastal Climatic Data Collection and Management. I worked as a back-end Android and Web application developer. In another part of the project, I worked on dimensionality reduction of the acquired dataset for quick vulnerability assessment of the coastal area people. I was appointed as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of North South University for the courses of "Data Structure and Algorithms", "Java Programming Language" and "Design and Analysis of Algorithms". I had also the opportunity to do an internship in the start-up Doctorbaari.com as a Front-end Developer.
